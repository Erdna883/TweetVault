# Privacy Policy for Twitter Bookmark Organizer

**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01

## 1. Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers.

*   **Bookmarks**: Stored locally on your device using IndexedDB.
*   **Authentication**: We do not handle passwords. The extension interacts with the Twitter/X session already active in your browser.

## 2. Data Usage
Your data is used solely for the purpose of organizing and displaying your bookmarks within the extension.

## 3. Third-Party Access
We do not share your data with any third parties.

## 4. Permissions
The extension requests the following permissions:
*   `storage`: To save your bookmarks locally.
*   `activeTab` / `host_permissions`: To scrape bookmarks from twitter.com when you request a sync.

## 5. Contact
For questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
